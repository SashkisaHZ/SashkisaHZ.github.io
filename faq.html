Frequently Asked Questions
How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You can find a printer in every corridor in the HZ. Connect to the printer and select either A3 or A4 format. There are also plotters located in L0-hallway and GW006.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

To scan a document, use one of the available scanners at HZ. Log on to the printer using your HZ card or by entering your username and password. Choose the desired scan option, place the originals on the feeder or glass plate and click Start. After scanning make sure to log off.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

To purchase items on the HZ web shop, visit the web shop website and browse through the different categories until you find the desired product. Add the item to your cart, enter payment information, and confirm your purchase.

How can you book a project space?

To book a project space, visit the HZ Self Service Portal. Click on Reservations and choose the desired category. Select the day and by dragging the left mouse button indicate the time period, click Next, check the reservation, fill in your details and submit it.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

For parking at the HZ parking lot, follow the parking regulations provided by the university. Ensure you have a valid parking permit if required, and park only in designated areas. Pay attention to any signage or instructions regarding parking rules and restrictions.

What should I do if I have lost my HZ-Card?

Student passes are issued by the student office. You can request a new HZ student pass via the Student Office (csa@hz.nl). The balance on your HZ card is linked to your account and after you have registered the card again you can simply pay with it.

How do I request rights to a shared folder?

Open an internet browser and navigate to https://hz.nl/secure. Fill in your HZ user name and password and click on Next, then Shared Folders. Click on My shared folder permissions, Permission request, select the rights you want to obtain, search the shared folder, select it and Save it. The rights have now been requested. After approval of the owner of the shared folder you will automatically receive an e-mail and you will have access.
