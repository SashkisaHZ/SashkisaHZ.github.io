Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

To print a document from your laptop at HZ, connect to the HZ printer network and follow the instructions provided in the HZ printing guide. Ensure you have installed the necessary drivers and have access to your printing credits.

2. How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

To scan a document, use one of the available scanners at HZ. Follow the on-screen instructions to scan the document and choose the option to send it via email or save it to a USB drive. You can then transfer it to your laptop.

3. How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

To purchase items on the HZ web shop, visit the web shop website and browse for the items or events you wish to buy. Follow the checkout process, which typically includes adding items to your cart, entering payment information, and confirming your purchase.

4. How can you book a project space?

To book a project space, visit the HZ booking system online. Select the desired date and time, choose an available project space, and complete the booking form. Confirm your reservation and you will receive a confirmation email with the booking details.

5. What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

For parking at the HZ parking lot, follow the parking regulations provided by the university. Ensure you have a valid parking permit if required, and park only in designated areas. Pay attention to any signage or instructions regarding parking rules and restrictions.
