  Technology in my life

It's not that my family revolves around technology that much. Actually, I was the first to be captivated by the beauty and coordination of the tech world around us, and with that, I've always strived to understand it better and to work with it. Living in an age of everyday inventions necessitates that people like me stay up-to-date with pretty much everything, which in a sense is another reason why I love technologies. Helping friends and family with my knowledge brings me happiness and even more drive to study and practice.
